Krad Miner

Krad Miner uses Firefox 4, WebCL and your video card (GPU) to earn you Bitcoin.

Krad Miner is 1,000 - 30,000 times faster than a Javascript based Bitcoin miner.



Update June 22, 2011 - Shut Down

Sadly due to denial of service attacks and hacking attempts I am shutting down Krad Miner. I have other big projects I am working on and I don't have any time to deal with such nonsense, and I have not been able to find anyone who honest and competent enough to take over running the site.

For those wondering, it took me about five full days of work to code Krad Miner, and the paltry cut that I took of the earnings doesn't come close to covering for the time investment. I love Bitcoin, and I hope those that earned some or got interested in mining from this site go on to be Bitcoin users for life.

I code open source software most days of the week, and I offered to open source Krad Miner, even dropping my offer down to 50 coins. (Charging open source money to open source some software sounds good enough to me..) But sadly the community couldn't come up with it. I got some big offers from Black hats that I turned down, and few offers for less than 50 coins from various people looking to 'start there own pool for newbies and grandma's using WebCL'. So unless a better offer comes along I have no intention to release this specific code, since it will likely just be used for pathetic rip off sites.
